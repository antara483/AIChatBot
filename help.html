Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a new conversation?

Click on "New Chat" in the sidebar menu to begin a fresh conversation. Your previous chats will be saved in the History section.

Can I upload Images?

Yes,You can upload images (JPG, JPEG) and get your queries answered.

How do I use voice input?

Click the microphone icon and grant browser permissions when prompted. Speak clearly and the AI will transcribe and respond to your voice commands.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption for all conversations and never share your data with third parties.
